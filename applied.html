Applied Sciences & Technology

The course covers research methods, material science, waste management, project management and total quality management, impart students with skills to understand develop and modify industrial technologies and therefore promote industrial development.

Our wide menu of courses enables learners to specialize in the areas that interest them most. The Faculty of Applied and Sciences and Technology is well positioned to teach its own programs, and to provide specialist services to the other Faculties that apply concepts and theories of science.
