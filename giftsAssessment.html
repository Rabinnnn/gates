ASSESSMENT OF GIFTS & TALENTS


How Gift and Talent Assessment is done


There are “Standard and Ad­vanced” test kits used to find out the ‘symptoms’ then pre­liminary tests are taken before making ‘confirmatory’ tests. The confirmatory tests are completed through a Gift and Talent Test aided machine; an Anthropo-biometric machine that uses safe white lights (not an x-ray machine) and used to produce Neural Body Models used by experts to confirm talents.

Steps in Gift and Talent Testing


First of all, find a centre for Gift and Talent Test near you and “Join The Programme”. Today Gift and Talent Test can be done remotely, thanks to the new “microchip” installed in special computers enabling it to seamlessly communicate with headquarters, hence geographical location does not matter.

Step 1: Oral Screening

Involves verbal chats to excite neutral domains and provide experts with “Inception Delimiters” of talent.

Step 2: Psycho-Screening

special engines with psycho-screen evaluation tools( in-depth AI and Data Mining) to improve and confirm Step1 values above.

Step 3: Anthropo-Biometric Screening

3D Optical Sensing Biometric Machine confirmatory Assessment to provide “body model” with “neutral channels” and bring “diametrics”.

Step 4: Expert Evaluation Reporting

Experts receive details of Step 1, 2, 3 and evaluate professionally and write reports detailing Learning Ability, Learning Products, Assessment Styles, with further useful recommendations as talent and related potential gifts and career forecasts.

Step 5: Learning Prescription

A Learning Prescription is further produced for the candidate and this is used by educators to support the learning of the candidate with a bias to Talent Development and Career Fulfillment.

Step 6: Admission and Support

A Learning Prescription is further produced for the candidate and this is used by educators to support the learning of the candidate with a bias to Talent Development and Career Fulfillment.


How long does it take to do a Gift and Talent Assessment?

It takes 30-40 minutes and results (reports) out within 24-48 hours.


How much does it cost to be assessed for Gifts and Talents?

It depends with your Membership status with the Federation as members receive 50% discounts

The following fees apply:

  • Step 1: Oral Screening – USD 50
  • Step 2: Psycho-Screening – USD 50
  • Step 3: Athropo-Biometric Screening – USD 100
  • Step 4 : Production of Talent Report = Outcome of Steps 1-3 (no payment)
  • Step 5: Production of Learning Prescription : USD 100

Other Steps above will depend on the institution (Talent or Academic Clinic) you will register in. This may include schools, colleges or universities that will be recommended as per your prescription.

Notes:

  • (a)Steps 1 and 2 are mandatory
  • (b) Step 3 may be recommended or desired based on “Preliminary Reviews & Talent ID”
  • (c) Step 5 is only required when joining Talent Based Learning programmes
  • (d) All fees paid are converted into your fees when you join Talent Based Learning in any of our Affiliate Institutions or Campuses or Schools.
  • (e) Amounts above depend on membership status. Members usually receive 50% discount while non-members pay the full amount.
  • (f)There are those that may be fully sponsored based on earlier approved requests.
