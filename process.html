The Process, Fees, and Duration

Step 1:

Submit C.V or have an oral discussion with our staff.

Step 2:

Details submitted into Special Computer-based Evaluation Engine (MATRIIC) which performs the actual evaluation of Life and Work Experience to extract “Credit Values” and provide current “Knowledge Value”.

Step 3:

Report is produced and interpreted by experts and shared with candidate.

Step 4:

(a) Course determined directly or through inherent Gifts and Talents (Unearthed during evaluation of Work and Life Experience in step 2 above).

(b) Reverse Engineering performed per request and Candidate Potential produce at age 8-10 years HENCE true and natural abilities (that could have been lost) determined even when at old age

(c) Based on (b) above candidate advised on natural career and ability that would inform on best business or enterprise to invest in. In other words, a businessman/woman, who wishes to invest in another business gets professional and scientific advice on the right business that is sure to succeed instead of losing hard-earned cash into an obscure enterprise.

Step 5:

(a) Accreditation and Credit Transfer takes place based on the Report in step 3 above.

(b) Candidate undertakes “Value Credit Transfer” based on 4(c ) above and completes a project in the form of new business that make him/her kill two birds with one stone. Get a new business under guidance of experts and also attain degree concurrently.

Step 6:

Student admitted at “Advanced Standing” which maybe:

  • Recommended Modules plus Dissertation = Bachelors, Masters or Doctoral Degree, depending on background.
  • Direct transfer through dissertation = Bachelors, Masters or Doctoral degree depending on background.
How long does the process of evaluation of life & work experience take?

It takes 30-40 minutes and results (reports) out within 24-48 hours

How much does evaluation of life and work experience take?

Members = USD 100

Non-Members = USD 200

However, whatever amount you pay will be converted into fees unless you are not taking the Experiential Learning with our Member Campus or Affiliate

The Evaluation at 4 (c) requires special arrangements and the fees depends on the nature of application. You will be advised by our experts upon request.
